How it works

Step 1

Go to one of the games in the Letterpress app on your phone.
Note
Make sure that your Letterpress theme is set to Light.

Step 2

Take a screenshot of your game.
How to screenshot
Hold down the hold and home buttons as the same time.

Step 3

Go back to letterpest and upload your board.
Note
Uploading only works in iOS6. This is not supported in other iOSs.

Step 4

All the words you can possibly play to beat your frienemies!
Plus
Search for words with specific letters.
Try it out by opening Letterpest on your phone today!
